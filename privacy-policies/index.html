Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 10th July 2025

Welcome to STAYLOO ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform to list or rent rooms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, payment information, list a room, or book a stay.
  • Property Information: Details about rooms or properties being listed, including address, photos, descriptions, and amenities.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and search queries.
  • Communication Data: Messages with our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform
  • Verify user identities and prevent fraud
  • Communicate with you about bookings, listings, and platform updates
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Between hosts and guests to facilitate bookings (contact information is shared only after a booking is confirmed)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a merger or acquisition

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

SSL encryption & secure servers

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your information
  • Objecting to or restricting certain processing
  • Withdrawing consent where applicable

You can manage many aspects of your information through contacting us.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and deliver personalized content. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

By using StayLoo, you consent to the use of essential and functional cookies. Manage preferences anytime via browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our platform or by email.
