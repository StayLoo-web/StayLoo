Frequently Asked Questions

Can I set my own rental terms (pricing, lease duration, house rules)?
Absolutely! As a landlord, you retain full control over rental terms. StayLoo provides agreement templates and recommendations, but you decide pricing, lease length, and house rules. Tenants see your conditions before unlocking.
Can I negotiate the rent or lease terms with the landlord?
Yes! Once you unlock property information, you can directly contact the owner and negotiate rent amount, deposit, lease duration, or any special clauses. StayLoo encourages fair communication.
How fast does StayLoo respond to listing requests?
Within 1 hour on WhatsApp! We get instant notifications and will reach out, assist with listing, or help book your dream room. Our support is quick and human.
Is there any subscription plan for tenants?
We simplified pricing: one-time unlock fees based on rent range. No monthly subscription — pay only when you need property details. Simple & transparent.
