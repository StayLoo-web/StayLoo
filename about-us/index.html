Our Story

Stayloo was born from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves a comfortable, hassle-free place to stay, whether for a night or a lifetime. Founded in 2025, we started as a small team of travel enthusiasts and real estate experts who saw firsthand how difficult it could be to find quality rentals that felt like home.

Frustrated by outdated booking processes, hidden fees, and impersonal stays, we set out to create a platform that puts people first. Today, Stayloo connects property owners with travelers and long-term renters through a seamless, transparent experience. From cozy city apartments to spacious vacation homes, we're committed to offering stays that inspire comfort, trust, and a sense of belonging.
