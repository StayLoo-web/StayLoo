About StayLoo

Connecting property owners with tenants. Our mission is to make property rental simple, transparent, and stress-free for everyone involved.

Our Story

StayLoo was born from a simple yet powerful idea: everyone deserves a comfortable, hassle-free place to stay, whether for a night or a lifetime. Founded in 2025, we started as a small team of travel enthusiasts and real estate experts who saw firsthand how difficult it could be to find quality rentals that felt like home.

Frustrated by outdated booking processes, hidden fees, and impersonal stays, we set out to create a platform that puts people first. Today, StayLoo connects property owners with tenants through a seamless, trusted process — built on transparency and mutual respect.
